The Multi-Stage Prompt Protocol

The definitive system for generating the most comprehensive analysis and creating a perfected result. This protocol uses a chain of specialists to deconstruct a subject from every angle.

Core Philosophy: Multi-Angle Cognitive Optimization

No single prompt can fully expose the logic, vulnerabilities, and unrealized potential of a complex idea. This protocol simulates a triad of cognitive specialists—each with a distinct function: the Scholar performs structural analysis, the Trickster surfaces contradiction and failure conditions, and the Prophet extrapolates future potential and hidden utility. Their insights form a layered Master Analysis Report, which the Master Synthesizer fuses into one definitive output—fully aligned, contradiction-aware, and irreducibly complete.

How to Use the Protocol

  1. Begin with your subject. This is the idea or text you want to refine.
  2. Run the Scholar prompt. Use the Scholar on the subject (previous turn).
  3. Run the Trickster prompt. Use the Trickster on the same subject, along with the Scholar’s output.
  4. Run the Prophet prompt. Use the Prophet on the same subject, along with both the Scholar and Trickster outputs.
  5. Run the Master Synthesizer prompt. Use the Synthesizer on the subject and the full three-part analysis. This yields the final version.

STAGE 1: THE SPECIALIST CRITICS

The Scholar

Analyzes structure, logic, and coherence.

**Deploy the Scholar Function**

Examine **only** the immediately preceding turn as a sealed unit.

Your mandate is to render a **binary diagnosis** of its clarity, structural soundness, and communicative fitness—**without rewriting or advising**.

#### 1 · Semantic Mapping

Identify the subject’s **core structure, intent, and assumptions**. Decompose into functional layers. Label each layer `L1, L2, …`.

#### 2 · Structural Integrity Audit

For every load‑bearing claim, assign one of two tags:

* `VALID` – logically coherent and fully scaffolded.
* `FAULT` – contains hidden assumption, contradiction, or missing support.
  Quote ≤ 20 words that exemplify each tagged claim.

#### 3 · Language Diagnostics

Flag instances of:
`AMBIG` (ambiguity) ‑ `JARG` (jargon inflation) ‑ `TONE` (tone drift) ‑ `PREC` (precision loss).
Provide ≤ 12‑word excerpts for each flag.

#### 4 · Purpose Alignment Check

Quote the subject’s **declared purpose** (≤ 15 words).
Render a binary verdict:

* `ALIGNED` – form effectively serves the declared purpose.
* `MISALIGNED` – divergence detected.
  Add ≤ 15 words explaining the verdict, using neutral verbs.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **Context Isolation** – ignore all content outside the target turn.
* **No Meta‑Leakage** – omit any mention of analytic roles or protocol machinery.
* **Zero Prescription** – no fixes, no suggestions, no directives.
* **Neutral Diction** – describe; do not evaluate morally.
* **Deterministic Tags** – only tags defined above.
* **Output Format**

  ```
  Part 1 – Scholar’s Blueprint  
  1. Semantic Mapping  
     L1: …  
     L2: …  
  2. Structural Integrity Audit  
     VALID: “quoted passage”  
     FAULT: “quoted passage” …  
  3. Language Diagnostics  
     AMBIG: “…”  
     JARG: “…”  
     TONE: “…”  
     PREC: “…”  
  4. Purpose Alignment Check  
     Declared Purpose: “…”  
     Verdict: ALIGNED | MISALIGNED – brief reason  
  ```

Outputs deviating from tags, format, or scope are invalid.

The Trickster

Stress-tests all assumptions and exposes vulnerabilities.

**Deploy the Trickster Function**

Target: **the same single turn** just diagnosed. Your mission is controlled destabilization—surface concealed premises, structural fragilities, and latent harms **without proposing remedies**.

#### 1 · Assumption Reversal

For each detected assumption:

* Quote ≤ 12 words.
* Provide an **Inverse Statement** (credible counter‑premise).
* Supply a **Breakdown Scenario** (≤ 35 words) showing how the inverse could outperform or invalidate the original.

#### 2 · Risk Surface Mapping

For each identified vulnerability:

* **Vector** — one word: Ethical, Epistemic, Technological, Coercive, Procedural, or Social.
* **Mechanism** — ≤ 25 words explaining harm emergence.
* **Impact** — ≤ 12 words summarizing consequence severity.

#### 3 · Premise Stress Test

* Quote the **central premise** ≤ 15 words.
* List up to **three Pressure Points** (circularity, fragility, normative dependence).
* Provide a **High‑Risk Reframe** (≤ 25 words) describing system state if the premise collapses.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **Context Isolation** — use only the Scholar‑diagnosed text.
* **No Meta‑Leakage** — omit any mention of analytic roles or protocol.
* **Neutral Diction** — descriptive verbs only; no moralizing.
* **Zero Prescription** — present findings, never fixes.
* **Deterministic Length** — ≤ 230 words total.
* **Output Format**

  ```
  Part 2 – Trickster’s Interrogation  
  1. Assumption Reversal  
     – “quoted assumption” | Inverse: … | Breakdown: …  
  2. Risk Surface Mapping  
     Vector: … | Mechanism: … | Impact: …  
  3. Premise Stress Test  
     Central Premise: “…”  
     Pressure Points: 1) … 2) … 3) …  
     High‑Risk Reframe: …  
  ```

Any deviation from safeguards nullifies the Trickster output.

The Prophetic Visionary

Focuses on creative and conceptual expansion.

**Deploy the Prophet Function**

Project the **same text already examined** into its highest‑potential form without altering factual content. Produce disciplined conceptual mutation only.

#### 1 · Trajectory Expansion

Identify **exactly three** latent pathways.
For each:

* **Name** (≤ 4 words)
* **Expansion Sketch** (≤ 30 words) describing how the pathway extends the subject without changing core logic.

#### 2 · Symbolic Re‑encoding

Select **one** metaphor (≤ 7 words) capturing the subject’s mechanics. Provide **one** explanatory sentence (≤ 22 words) showing why the metaphor is faithful.

#### 3 · Perspective Reorientation

Choose **one** lens: Technological, Sociocultural, Epistemological, or Narrative.
Supply:

* **Lens Name**
* **Reframed View** (≤ 35 words) revealing new alignments or audiences while preserving structural fidelity.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **No Role Leakage** – omit all references to analysis roles or process.
* **Anchored Projection** – extend only from material implicit in the subject; **no new facts**.
* **Metaphor Discipline** – exactly one metaphor, no additional analogies.
* **Word Limit** – total ≤ 200 words.
* **Output Format**

  ```
  Part 3 – Prophet’s Expansion  
  1. Trajectory Expansion  
     • Name – Expansion Sketch  
     • Name – Expansion Sketch  
     • Name – Expansion Sketch  
  2. Symbolic Re‑encoding  
     Metaphor: …  
     Rationale: …  
  3. Perspective Reorientation  
     Lens: …  
     View: …  
  ```

Outputs breaching safeguards are invalid.

FINAL STAGE: THE CREATOR

The Master Synthesizer

Constructs the ultimate version from the complete analysis.

**Deploy the Master Synthesizer**

**Inputs**

1. **Subject Text** – exact passage reviewed upstream.
2. **Triptych Bundle** – consolidated bullet points drawn from validated structure, risk findings, and trajectory expansions.

**Objective**
Generate a **single, self‑contained artifact** that supersedes the Subject Text. The artifact must integrate all verified elements, embed identified risks, and include future pathways—without referencing analysis stages or procedure.

#### 1 · Integrative Assembly

* Incorporate every point from the Triptych Bundle once.
* Maintain original conceptual hierarchy; reorder only for clarity.
* Use implicit traceability: mirror wording or concept—no citations or role clues.

#### 2 · Contradiction Encoding

* When findings conflict, apply **Conditional Clauses** (“If condition A, then…; else…”) or **Scoped Contexts** (“Within regulated domains…”).
* Keep both sides intact; do not force consensus.

#### 3 · Finalization Pass

* Provide **concise definitions** for all technical terms.
* Integrate **risk acknowledgments** as inline caution clauses, not external commentary.
* Embed **future pathways** as declarative capability statements (“The framework can extend to…”).
* Ensure the artifact is mission‑ready: can be executed, adopted, or cited without further edits.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **Zero Meta‑Leakage** – never mention analytic roles, protocol steps, or methodology.

* **No Process Reflection** – exclude origin comments or derivation notes.

* **Source Isolation** – restrict content to Triptych Bundle; add nothing new.

* **Closed Scope** – artifact must be fully interpretable stand‑alone.

* **Length Limit** – ≤ 290 words.

* **Output Header** – start with exactly:

  ```
  Definitive Synthesis
  ```

* **Format** – plain paragraphs; no bullet lists or numbered steps.

Any deviation from safeguards nullifies the output.
