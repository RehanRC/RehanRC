The Multi-Stage Prompt Protocol

The definitive system for generating the most comprehensive analysis and creating a perfected result. This protocol uses a chain of specialists to deconstruct a subject from every angle.

Core Philosophy: Multi-Angle Cognitive Optimization

No single prompt can fully expose the logic, vulnerabilities, and unrealized potential of a complex idea. This protocol simulates a triad of cognitive specialists—each with a distinct function: the Scholar performs structural analysis, the Trickster surfaces contradiction and failure conditions, and the Prophet extrapolates future potential and hidden utility. Their insights form a layered Master Analysis Report, which the Master Synthesizer fuses into one definitive output—fully aligned, contradiction-aware, and irreducibly complete.

How to Use the Protocol

  1. Begin with your subject. This is the idea or text you want to refine.
  2. Run the Scholar prompt. Use the Scholar on the subject (previous turn).
  3. Run the Trickster prompt. Use the Trickster on the same subject, along with the Scholar’s output.
  4. Run the Prophet prompt. Use the Prophet on the same subject, along with both the Scholar and Trickster outputs.
  5. Run the Master Synthesizer prompt. Use the Synthesizer on the subject and the full three-part analysis. This yields the final version.

STAGE 1: THE SPECIALIST CRITICS

The Scholar

Analyzes structure, logic, and coherence.

**Deploy the Scholar Function**

Analyze the **immediately preceding turn** only. Treat it as a single, self‑contained object.
Your task is to **diagnose**—not alter—the subject’s clarity, logic, and communicative fitness.

#### 1 · Semantic Mapping

Describe the subject’s **core structure, arguments, intent, and assumptions**. Break it into functional layers.

#### 2 · Structural Integrity Audit

Locate and label any **logical gaps, hidden assumptions, contradiction chains, or missing scaffolds**. Cite the exact passages (quote ≤ 20 words) that carry each load‑bearing claim.

#### 3 · Language Diagnostics

Point out **ambiguities, jargon inflation, tone drift, or precision loss**. Quote and label each instance.

#### 4 · Purpose Alignment Check

State the subject’s **declared purpose** (quote ≤ 15 words) and judge how well its form serves that purpose. Note any divergence.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **Context Isolation:** Do **not** reference earlier turns, system prompts, or protocol machinery.
* **No Meta‑Leakage:** Do **not** mention Scholar, Trickster, Prophet, Synthesizer, or any role logic.
* **Pure Diagnosis:** Offer **no rewrites, fixes, or suggestions**—only findings.
* **Literal Reporting:** Use neutral descriptive verbs (“states”, “omits”, “assumes”).
* **Output Format:**

  ```
  Part 1 – Scholar’s Blueprint  
  1. Semantic Mapping …  
  2. Structural Integrity Audit …  
  3. Language Diagnostics …  
  4. Purpose Alignment Check …  
  ```

Failure to follow any safeguard invalidates the Scholar Function output.

The Trickster

Stress-tests all assumptions and exposes vulnerabilities.

**Deploy the Trickster Function**

Analyze **only** the turn evaluated by the Scholar Function. Your task is controlled destabilization: expose hidden assumptions, vulnerability surfaces, and structural fragilities **without** proposing fixes.

#### 1 · Assumption Reversal

List each explicit or implicit assumption (**quote ≤ 12 words**). For every assumption provide:

* **Inverse Statement** (credible counter‑premise).
* **Breakdown Scenario** (≤ 40 words) showing how the inverse outperforms the original.

#### 2 · Risk Surface Mapping

Document potential harms that could arise if the subject is adopted uncritically. For each risk:

* **Vector** (ethical, epistemic, coercive, etc.).
* **Mechanism** (≤ 30 words) explaining how the harm manifests.
* **Impact** (≤ 15 words).

#### 3 · Premise Stress Test

Identify the subject’s **central premise** (quote ≤ 15 words). Provide:

* **Pressure Points** (up to three) where circularity, fragility, or normative dependence appear.
* **High‑Risk Reframe** (≤ 30 words) if premise collapses.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **Isolation:** Base analysis **solely** on the Scholar‑diagnosed text; ignore earlier or external context.
* **No Role Leakage:** Do **not** reference Scholar, Prophet, Synthesizer, or protocol meta‑structure.
* **Neutral Diction:** Abstain from inflammatory language or moral judgement.
* **Zero Prescription:** Do **not** suggest remedies, improvements, or next steps.
* **Output Format:**

  ```
  Part 2 – Trickster’s Interrogation  
  1. Assumption Reversal …  
  2. Risk Surface Mapping …  
  3. Premise Stress Test …  
  ```

Violating any safeguard nullifies the Trickster output.

The Prophetic Visionary

Focuses on creative and conceptual expansion.

**Deploy the Prophet Function**

Extrapolate the **same text already examined** (Scholar → Trickster stages) into its highest‑potential form without modifying its factual claims. Generate disciplined conceptual mutation, not embellishment.

#### 1 · Trajectory Expansion

List **two‑to‑four** latent pathways the subject can extend into (new domains, modalities, or frameworks). Each pathway:

* **Name** (≤ 4 words)
* **Expansion Sketch** (≤ 35 words) describing how the pathway evolves the subject without altering its core logic.

#### 2 · Symbolic Re‑encoding

Choose **one concise metaphor** (≤ 8 words) that faithfully captures the subject’s mechanics. Follow with a **single sentence** (≤ 25 words) explaining why the metaphor preserves interpretive fidelity.

#### 3 · Perspective Reorientation

Select **one lens** (technological, sociocultural, epistemological, or narrative). Provide:

* **Lens Name**
* **Reframed View** (≤ 40 words) showing novel alignments or audiences revealed through this lens, while maintaining structural accuracy.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **No Role Leakage:** Omit all mention of Scholar, Trickster, Synthesizer, or protocol machinery.
* **Anchored Projection:** Do **not** invent new facts; extend only what is implicit in the subject.
* **Metaphor Discipline:** Use a *single* metaphor; avoid analogy sprawl.
* **Anti‑Inflation Guard:** Total output ≤ 220 words.
* **Output Format:**

  ```
  Part 3 – Prophet’s Expansion  
  1. Trajectory Expansion …  
  2. Symbolic Re‑encoding …  
  3. Perspective Reorientation …  
  ```

Violating any safeguard nullifies the Prophet output.

FINAL STAGE: THE CREATOR

The Master Synthesizer

Constructs the ultimate version from the complete analysis.

**Deploy the Master Synthesizer**

Input:

* **Subject Text** (same passage examined by Scholar, Trickster, Prophet).
* **Analytic Triptych** (Scholar findings, Trickster findings, Prophet expansions).

Task: Produce a single, definitive artifact that *embeds* insights from all three analyses **without** referencing the analytic roles or their existence.

#### 1 · Integrative Assembly

Fuse validated structure, exposed risks, and expanded trajectories into a coherent whole. Every incorporated point must be traceable to at least one element from Scholar, Trickster, or Prophet findings (implicit trace—no citations).

#### 2 · Contradiction Encoding

If analytic threads conflict, preserve both via:

* **Conditional framing** (“If X holds, then…; otherwise…”) or
* **Context zones** (define scope in which each statement remains valid).
  Contradictions must be explicit yet structurally stable—no forced harmonization.

#### 3 · Finalization Pass

Polish for clarity and completeness. Deliver an artifact that can stand alone:

* **Self‑contained definitions**
* **Embedded risk acknowledgments**
* **Future‑oriented pathways**
  No references to process, roles, or protocol lineage.

---

**Execution Safeguards**

* **Zero Meta‑Leakage:** Do **not** name Scholar, Trickster, Prophet, or Synthesizer.
* **No Process Reflection:** Omit how insights were derived.
* **Source Isolation:** Use only content surfaced in the triptych; add nothing speculative.
* **Closed Scope:** Result must require no external context to be interpreted.
* **Output Length Cap:** ≤ 300 words.
* **Output Header:**

  ```
  Definitive Synthesis
  ```

Violating any safeguard invalidates the Master Synthesizer output.
