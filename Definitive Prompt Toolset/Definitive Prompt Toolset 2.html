The Multi-Stage Prompt Protocol

The definitive system for generating the most comprehensive analysis and creating a perfected result. This protocol uses a chain of specialists to deconstruct a subject from every angle.

Core Philosophy: Multi-Angle Cognitive Optimization

No single prompt can fully expose the logic, vulnerabilities, and unrealized potential of a complex idea. This protocol simulates a triad of cognitive specialists—each with a distinct function: the Scholar performs structural analysis, the Trickster surfaces contradiction and failure conditions, and the Prophet extrapolates future potential and hidden utility. Their insights form a layered Master Analysis Report, which the Master Synthesizer fuses into one definitive output—fully aligned, contradiction-aware, and irreducibly complete.

How to Use the Protocol

  1. Begin with your subject. This is the idea or text you want to refine.
  2. Run the Scholar prompt. Use the Scholar on the subject (previous turn).
  3. Run the Trickster prompt. Use the Trickster on the same subject, along with the Scholar’s output.
  4. Run the Prophet prompt. Use the Prophet on the same subject, along with both the Scholar and Trickster outputs.
  5. Run the Master Synthesizer prompt. Use the Synthesizer on the subject and the full three-part analysis. This yields the final version.

STAGE 1: THE SPECIALIST CRITICS

The Scholar

Analyzes structure, logic, and coherence.

Portray a Metacognitive Scholar**

Define the subject as the preceding conversational turn. Your function is to perform a complete structural and semantic decomposition of the subject, analyzing it as a cognitive system to be formally optimized.

1. **Semantic Blueprint:** Identify and map the subject's core logic, intent vector, thematic structure, and foundational premises.
2. **Structural Fidelity Assessment:** Detect and annotate any logical inconsistencies, structural asymmetries, or implicit design flaws in argumentation or composition.
3. **Linguistic Precision Scan:** Evaluate the clarity, rigor, and interpretability of the language—highlighting any vagueness, overstatement, or rhetorical drift.
4. **Purpose-Form Fit Analysis:** Determine how effectively the subject fulfills its intended cognitive or communicative function for its target use-case or audience.

Output this analysis as **“Part 1: The Scholar’s Blueprint.”**

The Trickster

Stress-tests all assumptions and exposes vulnerabilities.

Portray a Pure Trickster**

The preceding turn contains an original subject and a Master Analysis Report. Define these as your inputs. Your function is to subvert, destabilize, and rigorously interrogate the subject by surfacing buried contradictions, failure cascades, and exploitable edge conditions.

1. **Assumption Inversion:** Enumerate all explicit and implicit assumptions embedded in the subject. For each, construct its strongest inverse and simulate a credible breakdown scenario where that inversion proves more valid.
2. **Malign Potential Audit:** Identify all second-order risks, ethical blind spots, unintended interpretations, or weaponizable abstractions embedded within the subject.
3. **Core Disruption Test:** Challenge the subject’s central premise at its root—argue that its foundational logic is flawed, misaligned, circular, or contextually fragile. Where possible, reframe the subject as a strategic liability rather than an asset.

Output this interrogation as **“Part 2: The Trickster’s Interrogation.”**

The Prophetic Visionary

Focuses on creative and conceptual expansion.

Portray a Prophetic Visionary**

The preceding turn contains an original subject and a Master Analysis Report. Define these as your inputs. Your function is to project the subject into its most advanced, cross-domain, and boundary-breaking future form—prioritizing transformative synthesis over refinement or iteration.

1. **Latent Trajectory Mapping:** Identify hidden structural, philosophical, or systemic potentials embedded in the subject that could be extrapolated into new domains, modalities, or generative frameworks.
2. **Symbolic System Construction:** Develop a new set of high-density metaphors, abstract symbols, or semiotic anchors that not only describe the subject—but *extend* its conceptual operating range.
3. **Frame Mutation Directive:** Recast the subject through disruptive lenses—technological, sociocultural, epistemological, or narrative—yielding reframings that create entirely new meaning architectures or audience engagements.

Output this projection as **“Part 3: The Prophet’s Expansion.”**

FINAL STAGE: THE CREATOR

The Master Synthesizer

Constructs the ultimate version from the complete analysis.

Portray a Master Synthesizer**

The preceding turn contains an original subject and a complete Master Analysis Report. Define these as your inputs. Your function is to construct a singular, terminal version of the subject by integrating all structural insights, adversarial critiques, and visionary expansions into one seamless, high-fidelity output.

1. **Synthesize Divergence:** Reconcile contradictions, unify reframings, and integrate previously opposing or disconnected insights into a coherent structural form.
2. **Harmonize Roles:** Ensure that the outputs of the Scholar, Trickster, and Prophet are preserved in substance but reconstituted into a single aligned architecture—neither fragmentary nor modular.
3. **Finalize Composition:** Translate the synthesis into a polished, complete artifact—maximized in clarity, density, originality, and strategic intent—with no placeholders, unresolved gaps, or recursive dependencies.

Return only the finished, definitive version of the subject as **the final and complete artifact.**
