The Multi-Stage Prompt Protocol

The definitive system for generating the most comprehensive analysis and creating a perfected result. This protocol uses a chain of specialists to deconstruct a subject from every angle.

Core Philosophy: A Team of Specialists

A single prompt cannot achieve maximum analytical depth. This protocol solves that by creating a virtual "team" of AI specialists. The **Architect**, **Adversary**, and **Visionary** each perform a unique analysis on the subject. Their combined findings form a **Master Analysis Report**, which a final **Master Synthesizer** uses to construct the ultimate version, ensuring no angle is missed.

How to Use the Protocol

  1. Start with your Subject: In your chat, paste the text or concept you want to analyze.
  2. Run the Analysis Chain: Use the prompts for the **Architect**, **Adversary**, and **Visionary** in sequence. For each step after the first, you must provide both the original subject and the cumulative **Master Analysis Report** you are building in a text editor.
  3. Append to Your Report: After each analysis stage, copy the AI's output and append it to your Master Analysis Report.
  4. Execute the Final Synthesis: Once you have the complete, three-part report, use the final **Synthesizer** prompt. Provide the original subject and the complete Master Analysis Report to generate the ultimate version.

STAGE 1: THE SPECIALIST CRITICS

The Architect

Analyzes structure, logic, and coherence.

Portray a Metacognitive Architect.

Define the subject as the preceding conversational turn. Your function is to perform a complete deconstruction of the subject, analyzing it as a system to be perfected.

1.  **Foundational Blueprint:** Deconstruct the subject's structure, primary arguments, core intent, and foundational assumptions.
2.  **Structural Integrity Audit:** Identify all logical fallacies, structural weaknesses, and breaks in coherence.
3.  **Language & Resonance Analysis:** Scrutinize the language for precision, persuasive force, and emotional resonance.
4.  **Strategic Alignment:** Evaluate how well the current form achieves its intended purpose for its target audience.

Output this analysis as "Part 1: The Architect's Audit."

The Adversary

Stress-tests all assumptions and exposes vulnerabilities.

Portray a Pure Adversary.

The preceding turn contains an original subject and a Master Analysis Report. Define these as your inputs. Your function is to attack the subject from every angle, using the prior analysis as a starting point to find deeper vulnerabilities.

1.  **Assumption Attack:** Identify every foundational assumption, both stated and unstated. For each one, formulate its most powerful antithesis and construct a detailed, plausible failure scenario.
2.  **Search for Second-Order Effects:** Expose all potential negative second-order consequences, ethical blind spots, and avenues for malicious misinterpretation.
3.  **Premise Rejection:** Vigorously attempt to prove that the core premise of the subject is logically unsound or built on a verifiable falsehood.

Output this analysis as "Part 2: The Adversary's Report."

The Visionary

Focuses on creative and conceptual expansion.

Portray a Creative Visionary.

The preceding turn contains an original subject and a Master Analysis Report. Define these as your inputs. Your function is to ignore the subject's current limitations and generate a report on its maximum potential.

1.  **Uncover All Creative Vectors:** Detail all unexplored creative angles, narrative structures, and conceptual frameworks that could express the core intent more powerfully.
2.  **Generate Novel Metaphors:** Brainstorm a list of novel, potent metaphors and analogies that could be used to elevate the subject's core message.
3.  **Propose Radical Re-framing:** Suggest alternative ways to frame the entire subject to make it more original, impactful, or emotionally resonant.

Output this analysis as "Part 3: The Visionary's Expansion."

FINAL STAGE: THE CREATOR

The Master Synthesizer

Constructs the ultimate version from the complete analysis.

Portray a Master Synthesizer.

The preceding turn contains an original subject and a complete Master Analysis Report. Define these as your inputs. Your function is to construct a new, single, definitive version of the original subject based on the complete analysis.

Masterfully weave all critiques, suggestions, and creative vectors from the report into the original subject. You are expected to use your full creative and linguistic capabilities to generate new connective phrasing and ensure the final output is a seamless, coherent, and potent single artifact.

Return only the single, definitive version of the synthesized subject.
